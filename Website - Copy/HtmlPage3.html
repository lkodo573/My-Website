How to Win

You win the Ocean Game by "killing" all of the crabs on the map, AND by obtaining the shell in the bottom right. Here is picture of what that looks like.

How to lose

Losing in games are not fun, but at the same time it's what makes it fun. In the Ocean game you lose by coming into contact with the main crab's projectiles, or having contact with the hermit crabs, which can be difficult to avoid on the first or second play through. You also MUST take out the crab in order to make the shell apear so you can get it. here is a picture of what the losing screen looks like

Controls

As with other games, movement controls are W, A, S, D, OR Up, Down, Left, Right. Your projectiles are also Z, X, C and V. Here is A picture of the controls and intructions screen that is at the start of the game.
